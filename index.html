PointSav Digital Systems™ Introduces the "Digital First" Operating System for Real Estate Infrastructure

Vancouver, British Columbia - March 2, 2026 - Woodfine Management Corp. ("Woodfine") has introduced PointSav Digital Systems™ ("PointSav") as a new digital infrastructure subsidiary of Woodfine Capital Projects Inc. (the "Promoter"). [cite: 131]

PointSav serves as the foundational operating system architecture for Woodfine, its administrator and first customer. [cite: 132] PointSav is not a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform. [cite: 133] It is an operating system architecture designed to give building operators direct ownership, long-term security, and independent control over their physical assets and data. [cite: 133]

The Three-Track System

1. Private Network: A secure routing layer engineered to connect physical buildings and isolated data vaults to command centers without exposing data to the public internet. [cite: 137]

2. Orchestration: At the center of the system are distinct, independent data vaults known as Totebox Archives™. [cite: 138] For Woodfine, these are deployed in three primary configurations: Personnel Archives, Corporate Archives, and Property Archives, ensuring absolute data segregation. [cite: 139]

3. Workplace OS: A secure desktop environment that outputs deterministic, machine-readable files, not hidden database entries. [cite: 140]
